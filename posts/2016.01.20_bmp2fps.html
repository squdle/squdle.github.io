Animating to music.

2016.01.20

BMP 2 FPS

Traditional animators used to use music with a bpm that divided well into 24 frames per second. If you need to animate to music, you might want keyframes to hit on each beat. This can be a bit tricky when the numbers don't divide well. I wrote a small python script to help with this. It's far from perfect, but can be used to get a good starting point.
